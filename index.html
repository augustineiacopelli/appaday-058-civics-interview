CIVICS INTERVIEW PRACTICE

Modeled on the Official USCIS Civics Questions, 2008 and 2025 Versions

This session simulates the civics portion of the naturalization interview. Choose which version of the test to practice, then begin. Questions are drawn fresh at random each time.

Questions are adapted from the publicly published USCIS civics questions and answers. This tool is an independent study aid and is not produced, reviewed, or endorsed by USCIS or the Department of Homeland Security. Applicants who filed Form N-400 before October 20, 2025 take the 2008 test, ten questions from a pool of 100, needing six correct to pass. Those who filed on or after that date take the 2025 test, twenty questions from an expanded pool of 128, needing twelve correct to pass. Officeholder-specific and personal questions (current President, Governor, Senators, Representative, state capital, and similar) are intentionally excluded from both banks since those answers change over time or depend on where you live; study those separately from current official sources before an actual interview.
